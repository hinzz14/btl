A genuine footballing superstar, Neymar Jr quickly became a sensation in his home country of Brazil. He played 230 professional games for his first club Santos FC, scoring 138 goals before moving to Barcelona in Europe, where he scored an impressive 105 goals in 186 games. Neymar Jr won the Champions League in 2015 and the Olympic games in Rio in 2016, alongside his current teammates Marquinhos and Rafinha.



In 2017, the Brazilian signed for Paris Saint-Germain before then teaming up with another high-powered new arrival, Kylian Mbappé, determined to write history. A technical leader, a dribbling genius, an unpredictable detonator, the number 10 quickly sparked in the City of Light, helping the capital club to the Champions League final in the summer of 2020.
