BIOGRAPHY

Northern Ireland international who came through the ranks at the club and made almost 200 first-team appearances under Sir Alex Ferguson.


After leaving to join West Brom in 2015, he enjoyed success with Leicester City, winning the Emirates FA Cup in 2021.

Following his departure from the Foxes, he trained with the Reds and signed a short-term contract in order to feature in the pre-season for Erik ten Hag's men.
