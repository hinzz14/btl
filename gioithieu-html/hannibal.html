BIOGRAPHY

Midfielder signed from Monaco towards the end of the summer transfer window in 2019.


A highly technical performer, he appeared in the UEFA Youth League for the French side and has represented the country at youth level, before switching to join Tunisia's senior squad in May 2021 and making his international debut against DR Congo soon afterwards.

He made his United debut in the defeat to Derby County after receiving international clearance and gained experience in both the Under-23s and FA Youth Cup.

Showing talent and character in the face of some pretty stern challenges, his attitude has been praised by Under-23s coach Neil Wood as he battles to impose himself on games. Moving into a slightly wider role, he has flourished alongside some of the other creative talents in the Premier League 2 side and has been a regular despite continuing to still be eligible for the Under-18s.

The youngster signed a new long-term contract on 15 March 2021 and was named Denzil Haroun Under-23s Player of the Year, before making his first-team debut in the 2-1 win over Wolverhampton Wanderers on 23 May 2021.

Having played a part in Tunisia's Arab Cup and Africa Cup of Nations campaigns, Hannibal began training with United's first-team squad on a permanent basis under Ralf Rangnick and made late-season appearances against Liverpool and Crystal Palace.

He was also involved in the 2022 pre-season tour of Thailand and Australia, before joining Birmingham City on loan for the 2022/23 campaign. The Tunisia international scored once and assisted six times for the Blues, and was awarded the club's Goal of the Season award for his cleverly taken long-range free-kick against West Bromwich Albion in February.
