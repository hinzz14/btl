BIOGRAPHY

Fernandez is a left-sided defender who looks extremely comfortable in possession.


Signed from Real Madrid in the summer of 2020, the Seville-born defender scored on his Under-18s debut in a win over Burnley. He was soon fast-tracked into the Under-23s and continued to impress, finding the net again with a long-range effort in a win at Brighton.

In total, he made 20 appearances for Neil Wood's team in 2020/21, contributing five assists, and continued with the group on a permanent basis in 2021/22.

A stellar season saw him named Denzil Haroun Reserve-Team Player of the Year in May 2022, and he was included in the matchday squad by Ralf Rangnick for first-team outings against Chelsea and Brighton.

Alvaro joined Championship side Preston North End on loan for the 2022/23 campaign and he proved a hit at Deepdale, as the Lancashire club just missed out on the Championship play-offs.

Fernandez made 42 appearances in total for PNE and won the club's Young Player of the Year award, before returning to United for pre-season.
